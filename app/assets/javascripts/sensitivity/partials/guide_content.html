How to Use the Temperature Sensitivity Tool

  1. Enter the Information about your Building (e.g. name, location)
  2. Enter the name of the energy meter that you want to analyze
  3. Select the date range over which you want to analyze temperature sensitivity of your data
    1. Note: Start and end date must be within the date range of your CSV file
  4. Make sure your CSV is in the correct format
    1. If your CSV is not in the correct format, then the analysis will not work!
  5. Upload your CSV and click 'Get Results!'
  6. See how your energy data responds to temperature!
